How to Use DeepShield

Three simple steps to detect manipulated content in seconds.

1

Choose your mode

Select Text, Image, or Multimodal analysis. Multimodal combines both signals for the highest accuracy.

2

Upload your content

Paste text, drag-and-drop an image, or supply both. Supported images: PNG, JPG, WEBP up to 20 MB.

3

Enable Explanation

Toggle "With GradCAM" / "With Explanation" to see why the model made its decision via heatmaps and highlighted spans.

4

Read the verdict

DeepShield returns a clear REAL / FAKE verdict with confidence bars showing exact probability scores.
